FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about HeightMax products, shipping, and more.

Products & Supplements
Who is HeightMax designed for? +
HeightMax is designed for individuals aged 12–25 who are still in or near their natural growth phases. Results are most pronounced for teens and young adults whose growth plates remain open.
How long until I see results? +
Most customers notice improvements in energy and sleep quality within 2–4 weeks. Measurable height and posture improvements are typically reported after 3–6 months of consistent use.
Are there any side effects? +
HeightMax products are made from all-natural ingredients with no artificial additives, GMOs, or synthetic hormones. As with any supplement, consult your healthcare provider before starting — especially if you're on medication or have a pre-existing condition.
How do I take HeightMax capsules? +
Take 2 capsules per day with food. For best results, take one capsule with dinner and one right before bed — growth hormones naturally peak during deep sleep. Use consistently for at least 90 days.
Can I take multiple supplements together? +
Yes! Our supplements are designed to work synergistically. The Growth Stack bundle is specifically curated to combine products that complement each other safely. We recommend checking with your doctor if you have specific concerns.
Does HeightMax work after puberty? +
HeightMax is most effective for those still in active growth phases (typically ages 12–22). After growth plates close, supplements won't increase bone length, but our posture correctors and height boosters can still help you look and feel taller at any age.
Are HeightMax products FDA approved? +
Our products are manufactured in an FDA-registered, GMP-certified facility in the USA. Dietary supplements are not FDA-approved in the same way medications are, but our manufacturing standards meet strict federal guidelines.
Height Boosters & Posture
Are the shoe lifts noticeable to others? +
No. Our shoe lifts are slim, discreet insoles that fit inside your existing footwear. From the outside, your shoes look completely normal. The sock lifts are also indistinguishable from regular socks.
How long should I wear a posture corrector each day? +
Start with 15–30 minutes per day and gradually increase to 2–4 hours. Your postural muscles need time to adapt. We don't recommend wearing it all day as your muscles need to work on their own to build strength.
What shoe sizes do the lifts come in? +
Shoe lifts come in Small (US 6–8), Medium (US 8–10), and Large (US 10–13). Sock lifts are available in Small (US 5–7), Medium (US 7–10), and Large (US 10–13). Elevator Sneakers are available in full sizes 7–11.
